Re(al)SizeViewer displays SVG and PDF files at their true physical size on your Android device screen.

Use it for pre-print verification, checking product part dimensions, or viewing DIY blueprints at actual scale.

■ Key Features

• Real-size display of SVG / PDF files (100% scale using device DPI)
• Three operation modes:
  - Fullscreen mode: hides all UI elements for clean viewing
  - Alignment mode: drag to move, two-finger gesture to rotate
  - Calibration mode: pinch to adjust DPI correction factor
• Custom zoom factor input
• Background color selection (6 presets)
• Keep screen on option
• Optional sharing of device DPI calibration data

■ Who Is It For?

• Designers and engineers (pre-print size verification)
• Manufacturing (checking physical part dimensions)
• DIY enthusiasts (viewing blueprints at actual scale)
